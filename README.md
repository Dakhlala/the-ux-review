# The UX Review

> Raw, unfiltered thoughts on tech, design, and digital culture. No compromise, no filters, just pure truth.

A brutalist-style blog and community landing page built with pure HTML & CSS. Designed to cut through corporate noise with bold typography, high-contrast colors, and unapologetic layouts.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Brutalist Aesthetic** — High-contrast colors, thick borders, and bold shadows
- **Fully Responsive** — Mobile-first design that scales beautifully to desktop (im working on this part still ;)
- **Community Section** — Join the rebellion with benefits and call-to-action
- **Testimonials** — Social proof from industry rebels
- **Author Profiles** — Meet the team behind the content
- **Trending & Categories** — Easy content discovery
- **Performance-First** — Zero frameworks, zero dependencies, just fast HTML & CSS
